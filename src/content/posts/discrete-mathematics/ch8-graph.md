## Hall's Condition
$$
v_{1} \to v_{2} \implies \forall A \subseteq v_{1},\ \left| N(A) \right| \ge \left| A \right| 
$$
### 充分性
- Case I: For all nonempty $A$: $\left| A \right| < \left| N(A) \right|$
- Case II: There exists a nonempty $A$: $\left| A \right| = \left| N(A) \right|$
